Install: Find in Page

These instructions will help you add the Find in Page bookmarklet to Safari.

Step 1: Bookmark This Page

Tap the plus button at the bottom of the screen, then select Add Bookmark.

Then tap Save.

Step 2: Edit The Bookmark

Tap the Bookmarks button at the bottom of the screen.

Find the bookmark you just made ("Find in Page"). Tap Edit, then tap on the bookmark itself.

You now need to modify the bookmark URL. Tap the URL field (it begins with "http:"), then tap the "x" control. The URL field should now be empty.

Tap and Hold on the URL field until the Paste button appears, then tap on it

Step 3: Use The Bookmarklet

When you're surfing the web and want to use your new bookmarklet, just tap the bookmarks button, then tap Find in Page.
